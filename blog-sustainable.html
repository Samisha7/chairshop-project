Sustainable Materials

Eco-friendly furniture for a better future

At Lisbon Chair Co., we're committed to creating beautiful furniture that doesn't cost the earth. Our sustainable approach starts with responsible material choices and extends to every aspect of our manufacturing process.

FSC-Certified Wood

Sourced from responsibly managed forests that protect biodiversity and support local communities.

Organic Fabrics

GOTS-certified organic cotton and linen grown without harmful pesticides or synthetic fertilizers.

Recycled Materials

Up to 70% recycled content in our metal frames and plastic components.

Plant-Based Foams

Innovative foams derived from soy and castor oil instead of petroleum.

Beyond Materials: Sustainable Manufacturing

Renewable Energy

80% renewable energy powers our workshop with solar panels covering our production facility.

Water Conservation

Advanced recycling systems reduce our water consumption by 60% compared to traditional manufacturing.

Low-VOC Finishes

All our finishes and adhesives protect both workers and customers from harmful chemicals.

Waste Reduction

95% waste diversion from landfills through comprehensive recycling and composting programs.

Our Environmental Impact

Carbon Footprint

Looking Forward

Biodegradable Composites
