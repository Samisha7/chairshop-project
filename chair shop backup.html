Frequently Asked Questions

What is your shipping policy?

We offer free shipping on all orders over €200 within Portugal. For orders under €200, standard shipping is €15. International shipping rates vary by location and are calculated at checkout. Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days and delivered within 3-7 business days.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused chairs in their original packaging. Simply contact our customer service team to initiate a return. Return shipping costs are covered by us for defective items, while the customer is responsible for return shipping on change-of-mind purchases.

Do you offer warranty on your chairs?

Yes! All our chairs come with a comprehensive 5-year warranty covering manufacturing defects and structural issues. The warranty does not cover normal wear and tear or damage from misuse. Extended warranty options are available at checkout.

How do I care for my chair?

Care instructions vary by material. For fabric chairs, vacuum regularly and spot clean with mild detergent. For leather chairs, wipe with a damp cloth and condition every 6 months. For outdoor chairs, clean with soap and water and store during harsh weather. Detailed care guides are included with each purchase.

Do you offer assembly services?

Most of our chairs require minimal assembly and include clear instructions. For an additional fee, we offer professional assembly services in the Lisbon metropolitan area. Contact our team to schedule an assembly appointment.

Can I customize my chair?

Yes! We offer customization options including fabric/leather selection, color choices, and even engraved nameplates for corporate gifts. Custom orders typically take 4-6 weeks to complete. Contact our design team to discuss your specific requirements.
